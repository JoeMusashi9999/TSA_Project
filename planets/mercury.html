    Transport to Mercury is unavailable to the public at this time.

    Gravity:

    Gravity on Mercury is around 3.70 m/s². Compared to Earth's gravity at around 9.8m/s², Mercury has aroud 1/3 of the gravity of Earth.

    Weather:

    Because Mercury has no atmosphere, there is no discernable weather on Mercury.

    Habitation:

    Due to how close Mercury is to the Sun and how extreme tempratures are, there are no current plans on terraforming Mercury. Modern Scientists and space enthusiests have proposed a habitation ring around Mercury between the light and dark sides of mercury where tempratures are milder. However, no substancial reasearch has been put into how to implement this kind of habitat.

    Population

    As of now, there are no lifeforms alive on the planet Murcury.
